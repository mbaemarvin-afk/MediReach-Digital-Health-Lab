About MediReach Digital Health Lab

MediReach Digital Health Lab is a health innovation ecosystem focused on designing, testing, and scaling digital health solutions for Africa’s community health systems. We combine mobile technology, artificial intelligence, and data systems to improve access, continuity, and quality of care in underserved settings.

Mission

To transform community health through scalable digital and AI-driven health innovations.

Vision

A connected, intelligent, and proactive community health system powered by technology.

Approach

Human-centered design, low-bandwidth solutions, and real-world health system integration.

Innovation Focus Areas

We design, prototype, and test digital health solutions that solve real-world challenges in community healthcare.

Digital Health Systems

EMRs, telehealth platforms, and integrated digital care workflows.

AI & Predictive Health

AI-powered triage, symptom intelligence, and early warning systems.

Mobile Health Solutions

WhatsApp-based care, SMS health systems, and mobile-first engagement tools.

Community Health Intelligence

Data systems supporting CHPs and decentralized health decision-making.

Digital Health Research

Piloting and evaluating real-world digital health interventions.

Health Innovation Ecosystem

Partnerships, capacity building, and scalable African health innovation models.

Innovation Strategy

PHIN (In Development)

Predictive Health Intelligence Network — a system designed to use community signals and AI to support early detection and proactive health interventions.

WhatsApp Health Layer

A lightweight digital health interface enabling consultations, follow-ups, reminders, and community engagement via WhatsApp.
